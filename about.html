what is Oreycle

ORecycle is a brand owned by PT. Dibuang for the Technology-based Segregated Garbage Pick-up process which is implemented in the form of a digital application using a Smartphone. With the process through this application, your sorted waste will be picked up at your home, then weighed digitally, accurately, and in real time. We will replace the segregated waste in the form of rewards that can be donated to NGOs that work with us. For now, the types of waste that are managed by us are plastic, paper and glass bottles, and in the future there will be more types of segregated waste that we are ready to recycle. PT Dibuang is here to serve the community in waste processing as an effort to help in preserving the earth.
